HOW TO PLAY?

Players will take turns to place either X or O in a box by clicking on the box
Player 1 will start first, follow by Player 2
Player 1 will play as X, while Player 2 will play as O
If 'X' manage to get 3 in a row eiher horizontally, vertically or diagonally, Player 1 will win
If 'O' manage to get 3 in a row eiher horizontally, vertically or diagonally, Player 2 will win
If both symbols cannot manage to get 3 in a row, the game will end up as a tie
When the round is over click reset to play again
